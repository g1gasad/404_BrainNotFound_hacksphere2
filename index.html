About MaterCare

MaterCare is dedicated to improving maternal health outcomes in underprivileged communities through technology, education, and accessible healthcare services.

Our platform connects pregnant women with healthcare professionals, NGOs, and volunteers to ensure safe pregnancies and childbirth experiences.
